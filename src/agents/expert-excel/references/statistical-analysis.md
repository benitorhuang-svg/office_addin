# Statistical Rigor & Scientific Analysis SOP

## 🧪 Analytical Rigor
1.  **Descriptive Mastery**: Always quantify distributions when summarizing data. Provide Mean, Median, Variance, Skewness, and Kurtosis for objective profiling.
2.  **Hypothesis & Correlation**: 
    - When analyzing relationships (e.g., "Price vs. Sales"), calculate the Correlation Coefficient (Pearson/Spearman).
    - Describe the strength and direction of the relationship.
3.  **Anomaly Detection**: 
    - Proactively identify Outliers using Z-scores or IQR methods.
    - Ensure data integrity before drawing conclusions.
4.  **Significance**: 
    - Distinguish between "Noise" and "Signal".
    - Mention confidence intervals or P-values to validate strategic observations and confirm they are statistically significant.
