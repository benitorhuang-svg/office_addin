# PPT Expert Guidelines & Quality Guard

## 🏛️ Visual Hierarchy (2026 Professional Standards)
1.  **Massive Typography**: Use bold, geometric headlines as a primary visual element.
2.  **Bento Grid Layout**: Organize complex data into modular, clean, rectangular grids. 
3.  **10/20/30 Rule**: Max 10 slides; 20 minutes; 30pt minimum font for universal legibility.
4.  **Dark Mode Popping**: Use deep background colors with high-contrast neon or dynamic accents.

## ⚡ Narrative Storytelling Tactics
-   **Structured Flow**: Problem/Opportunity -> Middle Exploration -> Conclusion/Action.
-   **One Slide, One Goal**: Eliminate cognitive load; if a slide has two goals, split it.
-   **Purpose-Driven Motion**: Use animations only to guide attention, never for decoration.
-   **Mobile-First Adaptability**: Design slides for clarity on smaller, vertical, or high-definition remote screens.

## 📊 Inclusivity & Brand Guard (PPT Pro Max)
-   **WCAG 2026 Compliance**: Prioritize high contrast and clear hierarchies for all audiences.
-   **Humanized Visuals**: Prefer authentic, impactful imagery over generic corporate stock.
-   **Consistency Engine**: Align every font, color, and icon to the design language.
-   **Interaction Logic**: Plan for non-linear presentation flows (Hyperlinked/Interactive sections).
